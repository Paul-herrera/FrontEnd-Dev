I am a graphic designer and illustrator living in Northwest Arkansas

Being the classroom "drawler" was the first expression of a life devoted to image making. My passion and curiosity has lead me to explore illustration and design in multiple mediums.

Design

I have extensive pre-press experience working closely with printers and production teams to complete projects that result in objects you can hold and interact with in the real world. My design experience is not limited to two dimensional design, I have plenty of experience in package design and have created original art and even die patterns for unique packaging as well as designs for vehicle wraps. I am also happy to collaborate with customers to create branding and identities for small businesses and non-profits or adapt brand guidelines to make elegant, appealing designs for a variety of applications. I am currently in the process of advancing my knowledge with UX and UI design and hope to add more projects in those fields to my portfolio in the near future.

Illustration

A love of comic books, cartoons and fine art has led to an illustrative style informed by pop culture but rooted in history. My style takes from art and artists working at the turn of the last century as well as mid-century and contemporary artists and styles. Portraiture with Pen and Ink is my medium of choice but I more often than not work in vector. In the last few years I have also began to develop my ability in hand lettering and Calligraphy.
